Why a passport ?

In the latest Animal Crossing game, Animal Crossing New Horizons, you travel to your very own deserted island! But to travel... you need a passport! (And a Nintendo Switch.) However... not everyone has a switch. So we decided to let you dip the tip of a finger in this poetic world, by letting you create your very own customized Animal Crossing passport!
